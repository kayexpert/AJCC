About Africa Chamber Of Commerce

Who Are We And What Are We About?

TThe Africa – Jewish Chamber of Commerce (AJCC) was incorporated in Ghana as a business chamber dedicated to bringing together and leveraging an economic trade and investment opportunities between African businesses and their Jewish counterparts.

The Founder and Chief Executive Officer (CEO), Rev. Kofi Oduro Ofori – Agyeman Prempeh is a renowned Minister with decades of business collaboration and network with several Jewish entrepreneurs both in Israel and the diaspora.

Mission

The Chamber is dedicated to promoting business relations between African firms/companies and their Jewish counterparts for mutual benefits.

Vision

The vision of AJCC is to advance Africa Jewish business relationship for the economic, technological and social transformation of African countries.
